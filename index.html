The Coffee Shop

Welcome to The Coffee Shop, a locally owned café with three locations in Central Florida—North Lakeland, South Lakeland, and Winter Haven. We offer seasonal coffee flavors, delicious pastries from a local bakery, and a full menu to enjoy in our cozy café or to take on the go. Stop by to experience a warm, laid-back vibe and a true taste of the community!

Our Backstory

The Coffee Shop began in 2015 when two friends, driven by their love for coffee, decided to turn their dream into reality. For them, coffee was more than just a morning or afternoon pick-me-up—it was a daily ritual and a part of everyday life. What started as a cozy shop in North Lakeland has since grown into three locations, with the goal of expanding across Florida to share their passion for great coffee with even more people.

Fairtrade coffee

At The Coffee Shop, we are proud to serve 100% organic, Fairtrade coffee made from beans grown locally in Florida. Our beans are cultivated without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring a pure, rich flavor while protecting the environment and your health. Partnering with local, sustainable farms, we guarantee that our coffee is not only organic but also grown with care for the land and the farmers. By choosing Fairtrade, we ensure that these farmers are paid fairly, empowering local communities. Every cup of coffee at The Coffee Shop is a celebration of organic, ethical farming and fresh, local flavor.

Organic ingredients

At The Coffee Shop, our commitment to organic practices is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that by choosing organic, we're not only offering coffee that's healthier and free from harmful chemicals, but we're also supporting a more sustainable and eco-friendly way of farming. Organic farming enriches the soil, protects local ecosystems, and promotes biodiversity, all while preserving the natural flavors of the coffee beans. By prioritizing organic ingredients, we ensure that every cup you enjoy is not only better for you, but also better for the planet. At The Coffee Shop, going organic is more than a choice—it's our way of contributing to a healthier, greener future.

Locally owned

The Coffee Shop is proud to be a locally owned and operated business, rooted in the heart of our community. As a small, independent café, we are deeply connected to the people and local farmers who make our coffee possible. By staying local, we can provide a personal touch, ensuring that every customer feels at home while supporting the local economy. For us, being locally owned means fostering a sense of community and creating a space where everyone can gather and enjoy high-quality, responsibly sourced coffee.

Our Promise to the Community

Life gets busy, so let us fuel your day with the perfect cup—whether you're on the go or looking to unwind.
