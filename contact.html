Contact us!

At The Coffee Shop, we love connecting with our customers! Whether you have questions about our menu, want to place an order, or just want to share your experience, we’re here to help. You can reach out to any of our three locations in North Lakeland, South Lakeland, or Winter Haven, and our friendly team will be happy to assist you. We value every piece of feedback and are always excited to hear from our community. For general inquiries, event bookings, or catering options, feel free to email us at contact@thecoffeeshop.com or give us a call at (555) 123-4567. You can also drop by one of our cafés for a chat while enjoying a cup of our organic, Fairtrade coffee!
